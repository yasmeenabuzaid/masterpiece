About Blake Charles Salon

What you need to know about us!

As a contemporary salon located in San Francisco and Granite Bay, Blake Charles Salon is 100% committed to making you look and feel your absolute best! Our global team of experienced and talented stylists and estheticians will work with you to create a look that works for your personality and lifestyle.

Our cutting-edge hair salon in Union Square works on all types of hair. Whether you’re visiting us for a trim, balayage or a completely new hairstyle, we’ll make sure you achieve the look you’re after while you relax and unwind in our comfortable environment. We take great pride in our ability to cater to the needs of our diverse clientele, which includes busy parents, business professionals, students, young adults, fashionistas and everyone in between.

Once you step inside our hair salons, the Blake Charles experience begins and the outside world is left behind. Our focus is on you as we help you look and feel your absolute best so you can walk out the door with an extra spring in your step feeling like you’re ready to take on the world! We love meeting new people and are very adaptable with our approach to hair. Whether you’re a man or woman with thick, wavy, straight, curly or thinning hair, we welcome you into our salon as we routinely work with every hair type!
